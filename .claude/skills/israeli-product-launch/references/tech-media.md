# Israeli Tech Media Landscape

Reference guide for Israeli tech media outlets, pitch formats, and outreach strategies.

## Major Outlets

### Hebrew-Language Tech Media

**Geektime (גיקטיים)**
- Focus: Startups, product launches, developer culture
- Audience: Israeli tech community, developers, founders
- Best for: Early-stage announcements, product launches, community stories
- Pitch format: Hebrew email pitch, 3-4 paragraphs, subject line with key number
- Editorial calendar: Active year-round, slower during Tishrei holidays
- Contact: Editorial desk via website contact form or LinkedIn

**Calcalist Tech (כלכליסט טק)**
- Focus: Business and technology intersection
- Audience: Business professionals, investors, enterprise
- Best for: Funding rounds, exits, established startup milestones
- Pitch format: Hebrew pitch with financial data, company valuation context
- Editorial calendar: Strong coverage around Q4 funding announcements
- Contact: Tech desk editors via LinkedIn or direct email

**Globes Tech (גלובס טק)**
- Focus: Business, enterprise technology, fintech
- Audience: C-level executives, investors, financial professionals
- Best for: B2B launches, enterprise deals, fintech announcements
- Pitch format: Formal Hebrew, include market size and business metrics
- Editorial calendar: Active during business quarters, lighter in summer
- Contact: Tech section editors

**TheMarker (דה מרקר)**
- Focus: Economy, market analysis, larger companies
- Audience: Financial professionals, analysts, institutional investors
- Best for: Market analysis pieces, IPOs, major company news
- Pitch format: Data-heavy Hebrew pitch with market context
- Editorial calendar: Aligned with financial reporting seasons

**Walla Tech (וואלה טק)**
- Focus: Consumer technology, gadgets, mass market
- Audience: General Israeli public, consumer tech enthusiasts
- Best for: Consumer product launches, apps, gadgets
- Pitch format: Accessible Hebrew, focus on user benefit
- Editorial calendar: Active around shopping seasons (Black Friday, holidays)

### English-Language Israeli Tech Media

**CTech (Calcalist English)**
- Focus: Israeli tech for international audience
- Audience: International investors, global tech community
- Best for: Stories with international relevance, dual-market products
- Pitch format: English, include Israeli ecosystem context

**Geektime English**
- Focus: Israeli startups for global audience
- Audience: International VC community, tech scouts
- Best for: Global launches from Israeli companies
- Pitch format: English, emphasize the Israeli innovation angle

## Pitch Strategy

### Exclusive vs. Mass Distribution
- **Exclusive:** Offer one outlet first access for 24-48 hours. Best for major announcements (funding, exits). Results in deeper coverage and better placement.
- **Mass release:** Send to all outlets simultaneously. Best for product updates, event announcements. Lower individual coverage quality.
- **Tiered approach:** Give Hebrew exclusive to top outlet, then release English version to CTech/Geektime English separately.

### Timing Guidelines
- **Best days:** Sunday-Tuesday (beginning of Israeli work week)
- **Best hours:** 8:00-10:00 AM Israel time for Hebrew outlets
- **Avoid:** Friday afternoon (Erev Shabbat), holiday eves, Saturday
- **Embargo:** Israeli journalists generally respect embargoes; set clear lift time in Israel Standard Time (IST)

### Hebrew Press Release Template

```
[כותרת: מקסימום 15 מילים, לשון פעילה, מספר מרכזי]

[תת-כותרת: משפט אחד המרחיב את הכותרת]

[עיר, תאריך] -- [פסקת פתיחה: מי, מה, מתי, איפה]

"[ציטוט מייסד/מנכ"ל בגוף ראשון]," אמר/ה [שם], [תפקיד] ב[חברה].

[פסקת פרטים: תכונות, שוק, רלוונטיות ישראלית]

[פסקת נתונים: מספרים, לקוחות, צמיחה]

אודות [שם החברה]:
[פרשייה: 2-3 משפטים על החברה]

לפרטים נוספים:
[שם איש קשר]
[טלפון: +972-X-XXX-XXXX]
[אימייל]
```
